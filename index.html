The Chilean Quiltro

The quiltro is the typical abandoned mixed-race dog you can see on the streets of Chile. It is the loyal companion of the homeless people who need affection in this cold-hearted world we live in. If yoy happen to encounter one of these lovely creatures when walking daown the road, please give them a piece of bread or whatever you're eating at the time. The will reward you with swinging of its tail and a pretty thankful face.
